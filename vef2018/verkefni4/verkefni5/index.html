    • Brandon Semenuk

    Brandon Semenuk is a Canadian freeride mountain biker from Whistler, British Columbia. Semenuk is a 3-time FMB World Tour Gold Medalist. He is a 2013 Munich X-Games Silver medalist in the Mountain Bike Slopestyle event, a 2008 and 2016 Red Bull Rampage winner and a 5-time Red Bull Joyride winner. He is known as one of the best freeride mountain bikers in history.

    Brett Rheeder

    Brett Rheeder (born February 16, 1993) is a professional freeride mountain bike rider from Mount Albert, Ontario. Rheeder is a 2013 Munich X-Games Slopestyle Gold Medalist. In 2016 he won the Red Bull Joyride for the first and only time in his career.

    Brendan Fairclough

    Affectionately known as mountain biking's premiere “free-racer”, Brendan Fairclough is as comfortable making his way down a World Cup race track as he is back-flipping a canyon gap at Rampage. He has a way of having fun on anything with 2-wheels and whether he is seat bouncing his moto or playing on his fixie there is usually a smile on the Englishman’s face.
