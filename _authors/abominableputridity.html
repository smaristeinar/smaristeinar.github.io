--- short_name: Abominable Putridity name: Abominable Putridity position: Brutal slamming deathmetal ---

Abominable Putridity is a Russian slam death metal band originally from Moscow. The band has released two albums; In the End of Human Existence and The Anomalies of Artificial Origin. They are signed to the California indie label Unique Leader Records and are primarily known for their extensive use of 'destructive' slams and grooves in their guitarwork.
